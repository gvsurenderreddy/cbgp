[1mNAME[0m
     [1mbgp assert peerings-ok [22m-- check BGP peerings

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
     [1mpeerings-ok[0m

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
     This command checks that all the peerings defined in all the routers are
     valid, i.e. for each peering, the existence of the peer is checked as
     well as the existence of a similar peering definition in the peer.
