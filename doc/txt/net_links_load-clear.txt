[1mNAME[0m
     [1mnet links load-clear [22m-- clear the load of all links

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
     [1mload-clear[0m

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
     This command can be used to clear the load of every link/interface in the
     network topology.

[1mSEE ALSO[0m
     You can use the [1mnet node record-route [22mcommand with the [4m--load[24m option to
     put the provided amount of "traffic" on every link/interface traversed by
     the record-route probe.
