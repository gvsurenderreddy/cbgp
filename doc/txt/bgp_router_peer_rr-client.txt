[1mNAME[0m
     [1mbgp router peer rr-client [22m-- configure a BGP neighbor as route-reflector
     client

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
     [1mrr-client[0m

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
     This command configures one BGP neighbor as a route-reflector client. The
     router where the BGP session is defined implicitly becomes a route-
     reflector.
