[1mNAME[0m
     [1mnet domain show nodes [22m-- show the nodes that belong to one domain

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
     [1mnodes[0m

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
     This command lists the nodes that belong to one domain.

     Example:


     cbgp> net domain 1 show
     0.1.0.0
     0.1.0.1
