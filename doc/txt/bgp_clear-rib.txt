[1mNAME[0m
     [1mbgp clear-rib [22m-- clear the routes from all the routers

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
     [1mclear-rib[0m

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
     This command clears the routes from all the routers. That means that
     every router's Loc-RIB and Adj-RIBs are emptied.

[1mSEE ALSO[0m
     A similar command clears the routing Adj-RIBs but keeps the content of
     the Loc-RIBs: [1mbgp clear-adj-rib [22m.
