[1mNAME[0m
     [1mbgp assert reachability-ok [22m-- check the reachability of BGP prefixes

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
     [1mreachability-ok[0m

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
     This command checks that all the prefixes announced by each BGP routers
     are reachable by all the other BGP routers.
