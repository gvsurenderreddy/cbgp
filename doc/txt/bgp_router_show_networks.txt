[1mNAME[0m
     [1mbgp router show networks [22m-- list the locally originated networks

[1mSYNOPSIS[0m
     [1mnetworks[0m

[1mDESCRIPTION[0m
     This command shows the list of networks originated by this router.

[1mSEE ALSO[0m
     The command [1madd network [22mcan be used to add networks to this list.
